Becoming a Waterloo Hybrid Member

The very simple answer to – “What can I do for Waterloo Hybrid?”.

Anything you want. Waterloo Hybrid is a cross-disciplinary engineering team with members from Electrical, Mechanical, Computer, System Design and even Civil Engineering. We are always looking for new members to get involved who have a passion for learning new things. We do not expect you to have the knowledge to jump right into the most extensive engineering challenges. But if you are eager to learn and develop your skill set, we will help you as much as we can through the Formula Hybrid project so that you can reach your ultimate potential. And along with your success, you will become an intricate and important part of Waterloo Hybrid.

Waterloo Hybrid is not just a team for engineering students. With help from Arts and Business students, we expand our focus to sponsorship, media, management and outreach.

For more information, send us an email at uwaterloohybrid@gmail.com or stop by our work bay located on the first floor of Engineering 5. Look for E5 on the Campus Map of the university.

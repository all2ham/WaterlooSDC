About

Formula Hybrid is a design and engineering challenge for university students held annually during the spring at the New Hampshire Motor Speedway. The goal is to design, build, and compete an open-wheel, single-seat, electric or hybrid-electric race car. This car must conform to a formula which emphasizes innovation and efficiency.

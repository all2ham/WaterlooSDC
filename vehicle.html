The Car

Our vehicle has been designed with efficiency and sustainability as the primary focus. Furthermore, our vehicle has the unique ability to operate as either an electric-only or gas-electric hybrid vehicle. This ensures that our vehicle will have an extremely small carbon footprint without sacrificing the performance characteristics required of any race vehicle. Ultimately, our vehicle strives to be a synergistic implementation of hybrid vehicle systems on the cutting edge of automotive technologies.

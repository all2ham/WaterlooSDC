Become a Sponsor

Waterloo Hybrid is always looking to make new partnerships to help us develop, construct and reach our goal of a reliable and efficient race vehicle. Click here to meet some of our fantastic sponsors, who with their generous support, are helping us reach our goals.

Benefits for our Partners

  • Our partners gain valuable exposure at all Waterloo Hybrid events and competitions through the placement of their corporate logo on the race vehicle, team attire, and media.
  • Access and exposure to a diverse student body with great potential to be hired by top employers in both co-op and full-time opportunities
  • Exposure to industrial and academic visitors of the Univerisity of Waterloo Student Design Centre.
  • Opportunity to network with other Waterloo Hybrid sponsors and industry partners at community events
  • Opportunity to showcase the Waterloo Hybrid vehicle and team at corporate events

Please review our Sponsorship Package to find out more. Check out our Sponsor Recognition page for detailed recognition of each of our sponsorship levels. Contact Us to receive more information on how to become a part of this unique opportunity.
